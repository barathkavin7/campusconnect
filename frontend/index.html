Questions, answered

Everything you need to know.

Who can create a CampusConnect account?+

Current students and verified recruiters can register. Every account is reviewed by the college placement office before activation.

How is the student ranking calculated?+

The score combines CGPA at 40%, resume score at 35%, skills at 15%, and certificates at 10% for a transparent, balanced ranking.

Are resumes and company documents secure?+

Files are type-checked, size-limited, stored outside the database, and only made available through authorized workflows.

Does chat happen in real time?+

Yes. Students and recruiters can communicate instantly through authenticated Socket.IO channels, while messages remain available as a conversation history.
